Questions & Answers

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our health, Medicare, and life insurance processes.

Anyone enrolled in Medicare Part A and Part B is eligible to purchase a Medicare Advantage or Supplement plan. The primary enrollment window occurs around your 65th birthday, starting three months before and ending three months after. We can help you compare plans to find the right coverage.
Final expense insurance is a type of whole life coverage designed specifically to pay for end-of-life expenses, such as funeral costs, medical bills, or outstanding debt. It is ideal for seniors looking to secure peace of mind and protect their families from immediate financial burdens.
Setting up group insurance starts with sharing basic details about your business size, budget, and coverage priorities. We build customized plans matching multiple carrier rates and handle employee onboarding so your business transition is completely seamless.
