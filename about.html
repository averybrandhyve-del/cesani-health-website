About Jeremiah Cesani

A Mission to Serve & Protect

My name is Jeremiah Cesani, and my passion in life has always been to help and serve others. Having spent 12 years working with some of the most respected health insurance companies and insurance brokers in the country, I founded Cesani Health to provide a service that people can truly trust—built on authenticity, sincerity, and genuine appreciation.

Creating a Better Insurance Experience

I know firsthand how frustrating the traditional insurance process can be. Too often, people are transferred a dozen times, left uneducated by agents, misled, or simply ignored by customer service representatives. I wanted to create a genuine solution to these problems.

At Cesani Health, my team and I are dedicated to serving you at the highest level. We offer a true "one-stop-shop" experience. With access to over 26 top-rated carriers, we provide the peace of mind that comes with finding a plan tailored specifically to your financial and medical needs.

Our Commitment to You

We go far beyond standard customer care. Our team is available 24/7 to assist you with any questions, concerns, or issues that may arise. For us, this is about much more than just earning your business—it is about building a lasting relationship based on honor and trust. Let me serve you.
