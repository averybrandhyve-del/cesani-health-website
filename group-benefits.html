Group Health Insurance & Employee Benefits

In today's competitive job market, offering a robust employee benefits package is critical to attracting and retaining top-tier talent. Small and medium-sized businesses face unique challenges in balancing comprehensive health coverage with budget predictability. At Cesani Health, we partner with employers to design customized health insurance plans that satisfy both objectives.

Why Offer Group Health Benefits?

Providing employee healthcare packages carries significant advantages for your company:

  • **Talent Retention**: Healthcare is consistently rated as the most important benefit by job candidates.
  • **Tax Incentives**: Employer contributions to group premiums are generally 100% tax-deductible as business expenses. Furthermore, employees can pay their share of premiums with pre-tax dollars.
  • **Lower Group Rates**: Insuring a group typically costs less per person than individual plans because the carrier's risk is spread across the pool.
  • **Productive Workforce**: Direct access to preventative care, wellness incentives, and mental health support keeps your team healthy and reduces sick days.

Flexible Benefit Customization

We can help you assemble an integrated suite of benefits, selecting the tiers that best match your team's size and needs:

**Medical Insurance**: HMO, PPO, or HDHP (High Deductible Health Plans) paired with Health Savings Accounts (HSAs) to provide flexible copays and deductibles.

**Ancillary Benefits**: Dental and vision coverage, group life insurance, and short-term or long-term disability protection.

How the Quote and Setup Process Works

Our independent advisors handle the setup process from end-to-end to minimize friction for your HR team:

  1. **Census Collection**: We gather a simple listing of employee ages, zip codes, and coverage levels.
  2. **Market Comparison**: We aggregate quotes across major group health networks including United Healthcare, Aetna, Humana, Elevance, and Blue Cross Blue Shield.
  3. **Plan Modeling**: We help you determine the employer/employee premium split (e.g. 50/50, 70/30) to meet group size participation rules.
  4. **Seamless Onboarding**: We manage employee digital enrollment meetings, coordinate ID cards, and set up billing accounts.
