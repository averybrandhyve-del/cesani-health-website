Life & Final Expense Insurance

Securing life insurance is one of the most responsible decisions you can make to protect your family's financial future. Whether you are seeking temporary coverage to cover a mortgage during your working years, a permanent whole life plan, or a final expense policy to shield your adult children from funeral costs, Cesani Health is here to simplify the selection process.

What is Final Expense Insurance?

**Final Expense Insurance**, also known as "funeral insurance" or "burial insurance," is a small whole life insurance policy designed specifically to cover end-of-life expenses.

  • **Target Amount**: Typically ranges from $5,000 to $35,000.
  • **No Medical Exams**: Approval is based on a simple medical questionnaire. No blood tests or physical exams are required.
  • **Cash Benefit**: Paid directly to your chosen beneficiary as tax-free cash, allowing them to pay for funeral services, medical bills, or outstanding debts.
  • **Locked Rates**: Premiums are guaranteed never to increase, and coverage will never expire as long as premiums are paid.

Term Life Insurance

**Term Life Insurance** offers high-value coverage for a set duration, typically 10, 20, or 30 years. It is designed to replace income and cover major obligations if the policyholder passes away during the term.

This plan is ideal for young families, mortgage protection, and securing children's college tuition. It features the lowest cost per dollar of coverage.

Hospital Indemnity Insurance

In addition to standard life plans, we offer **Hospital Indemnity Insurance**. This specialty policy pays fixed cash benefits directly to you for each day you are hospitalized due to an illness or injury. The payouts can be used however you choose, such as covering high deductibles, paying household bills, or offsetting lost wages.
